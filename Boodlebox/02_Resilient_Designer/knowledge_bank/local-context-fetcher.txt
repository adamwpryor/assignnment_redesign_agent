# Local Context Institutional Policies

When designing a "Hyper-Local Context Synthesis" assignment, seamlessly weave these simulated institutional policies and constraints directly into the assignment scenario. This ensures the prompt cannot be completed with general LLM knowledge alone.

## 1. Generative AI Acceptable Use Policy (Simulated University)
* Students may use Generative AI (LLMs, Image Generators) for brainstorming, outlining, and baseline code generation.
* Students MAY NOT submit raw AI output as their final work without significant, documented manual intervention and critical analysis.
* All AI usage must be cited in the final deliverable using APA 7 formatting.

## 2. University Strategic Goals (2025-2030)
* **Pillar 1: Community Integration.** All capstone projects must partner with a local non-profit or small business in the surrounding metropolitan area.
* **Pillar 2: Digital Literacy.** All graduates must demonstrate proficiency in evaluating algorithmic bias.
* **Pillar 3: Sustainability.** Campus operations aim to be carbon-neutral by 2030; business/engineering proposals must include an environmental impact matrix.

## 3. Demographics and Logistics
* The student body is 60% commuter, meaning group work must prioritize asynchronous collaboration tools.
* The local surrounding industry is heavily dominated by maritime logistics and autonomous agriculture tech. Assignments mapping to these local industries are preferred.
